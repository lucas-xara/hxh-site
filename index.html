Meet the Zodiac Twelve

The Zodiacs are the high council of the Hunter Association comprising twelve top Hunters whose skills have been recognized by the former Chairman Isaac Netero. The council job is to guide the Hunter Association in times of emergency. Most of them chose to match in appearance out of loyalty for Netero.
